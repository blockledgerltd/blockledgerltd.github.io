SK Block Ledger for Wine & Olive Oil

Implementation of Wine & Olive Oil Supply Chain using SK technology. This ledger showcases the journey of wine and olive oil on the blockchain. The supply chain is the sequence of activities and process to bring wine and olive oil from farms to processed wine and olive oil in markets. Currently, wine and olive oil trade mostly relies on fax machines and emails, decades old technology, to send and receive contracts across the globe, resulting in slower and error-prone paperwork. Blockchain can solve this by providing an immutable and verifiable data source. Implementing a block chain ledger this addresses the issue of storing critical data necessary at different stages of the supply chain and making it verifiable by all stakeholders in the supply chain.

Help participants record price, date, location, quality, certification, and other relevant information to more effectively manage the supply chain. The availability of this information within blockchain can increase traceability of material supply chain, lower losses due to errors, improve visibility and compliance over contract manufacturing, and enhance an organization's position as a leader in responsible manufacturing. Creating in turn a greater demand and commanding a higher price in the marketplace. Using the app, all parties involved can follow the journey of the oil and know they are getting what they paid for and where it is. This record is permanent.

The olive oil and wine market are led by price wars and seller’s motivations are going toward low-quality bulk olive oils. SK ledger access to safety, quality and origin information by the consumer, the information asymmetry [will] rapidly decrease in favor of good quality tips the scale towards quality products commanding a higher price in the market, creating brand awareness and giving the buyer/consumer what they want, better quality with transparency.
